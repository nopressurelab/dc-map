<h1>Timeline, 2020 to 2026</h1>
<p class="lede">How Aragón went from a first environmental clearance to €47 billion in committed investment, a first-in-Spain datacenter lawsuit, and a Guardia Civil corruption case. Each entry links to the source.</p>

<div class="timeline">
<div class="tl-event tag-permit">
<div class="tl-date">27 July 2020</div>
<div class="tl-title">First INAGA environmental clearance for AWS</div>
<div class="tl-body">INAGA issues DIA and integrated environmental authorisation for the AWS datacenter site in the Plataforma Logística Sur de Huesca (PLHUS). The pattern is set: environmental processing tracks well ahead of construction.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.boa.aragon.es/cgi-bin/EBOA/BRSCGI?CMD=VEROBJ&MLKOB=1406588900808" target="_blank">Source (BOA)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-permit">
<div class="tl-date">2021</div>
<div class="tl-title">First PIGA for AWS (Amazon Data Services Spain)</div>
<div class="tl-body">The Aragón government declares the AWS project a Proyecto de Interés General de Aragón, bypassing municipal urbanism plans. This is the template every subsequent hyperscaler and speculative developer will use.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.aragon.es/-/proyecto-de-interes-general-de-aragon-sobre-la-implantacion-de-centros-de-datos-en-la-comunidad-autonoma-de-aragon-a-propuesta-de-amazon-data-services-spain" target="_blank">Source (Gobierno de Aragón)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-op">
<div class="tl-date">November 2022</div>
<div class="tl-title">AWS eu-south-2 (Spain) region goes live</div>
<div class="tl-body">The first three AWS datacenters, Villanueva de Gállego, El Burgo de Ebro, and Zaragoza, come online. Aragón&apos;s cloud-region reputation is now real.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.elespanol.com/aragon/economia/20260322/aragon-convierte-capital-datos-nube-calendario-amazon-microsoft-proyectos/1003744178360_0.html" target="_blank">Source (El Español)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-expansion">
<div class="tl-date">May 2024</div>
<div class="tl-title">AWS announces €15.7B Aragón expansion</div>
<div class="tl-body">Amazon announces what it calls its largest technology investment in Spanish history. This figure is later revised upward to €33.7B and expanded to 11 campuses across three provinces by 2031.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.hoyaragon.es/articulo/noticias-aragon/aws-centros-datos-aragon-piga/20260325132743125802.html" target="_blank">Source (Hoy Aragón)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-permit">
<div class="tl-date">3 July 2024</div>
<div class="tl-title">Microsoft PIGA declared for Villamayor de Gállego</div>
<div class="tl-body">The Aragón government declares Microsoft&apos;s Villamayor campus a Proyecto de Interés General. The full PIGA covers three campuses, La Muela, Villamayor and Zaragoza, and €5.356B in investment.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.aragon.es/-/piga-microsoft" target="_blank">Source (Gobierno de Aragón)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-permit">
<div class="tl-date">29 October 2024</div>
<div class="tl-title">Box2Bit Cariñena declared &quot;interés autonómico&quot;</div>
<div class="tl-body">A €3.4B, 5-building speculative campus in Cariñena promoted by Spanish independent Box2Bit gets fast-track status. Announced as one of the largest Spanish independent datacenter projects.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.aragonhoy.es/consejos-de-gobierno/declarada-interes-autonomico-interes-general-inversion-empresa-box2bit-carinena-97855" target="_blank">Source (Aragón Hoy)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-expansion">
<div class="tl-date">February 2025</div>
<div class="tl-title">Iberdrola signs 476 MW PPA package with Amazon (Iberia)</div>
<div class="tl-body">Three power-purchase agreements totalling 476 MW across Spain and Portugal. Amazon becomes one of Iberdrola&apos;s largest renewable offtakers.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.iberdrola.com/label/ppas" target="_blank">Source (Iberdrola)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-expansion">
<div class="tl-date">13 March 2025</div>
<div class="tl-title">Azora announces &quot;Tillion Aragón&quot; (€2B, 150→300 MW)</div>
<div class="tl-body">Spanish asset manager Azora enters datacenters through its Tillion vehicle with a Villamayor de Gállego site. No anchor tenant disclosed.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.aragonhoy.es/presidencia-de-gobierno/azora-levantara-centro-datos-villamayor-gallego-inversion-inicial-1-100-millones-euros-99309" target="_blank">Source (Aragón Hoy)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-scandal">
<div class="tl-date">5–6 August 2025</div>
<div class="tl-title">INAGA exempts AWS from ordinary EIA for 320+ ha</div>
<div class="tl-body"><strong>The smoking gun of the expedited-authorisation thesis.</strong> INAGA rules that AWS&apos;s 320-hectare expansion across four municipalities does not require an ordinary environmental impact evaluation, only &quot;preventive, corrective and supplementary measures&quot;. Justification: &quot;afecciones poco significativas&quot;. The same shortcut had been used for Stellantis&apos; battery gigafactory Phase 0.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.elespanol.com/aragon/economia/20250805/aragon-exime-amazon-evaluacion-ambiental-urbanizar-hectareas-centros-datos/1003743874714_0.html" target="_blank">Source (El Español)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-permit">
<div class="tl-date">15 September 2025</div>
<div class="tl-title">Vantage Data Centers announces €3.2B Zaragoza campus</div>
<div class="tl-body">US operator Vantage enters Spain via a 40-ha campus in Villanueva de Gállego, promoted by Spanish alliance Desarrollos Ecoindustriales La Cartuja and a Luxembourg vehicle F2 Dino Properties Sàrl. 90 MW guaranteed by Endesa for phase 1.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://bebeez.eu/2025/09/18/vantage-enters-spain-to-invest-e3-2-billion-in-zaragoza-data-center-campus/" target="_blank">Source (BeBeez)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-permit">
<div class="tl-date">18 September 2025</div>
<div class="tl-title">Vantage receives Declaración de Interés Autonómico (ORDEN PEJ/1264/2025)</div>
<div class="tl-body">The regional government declares Vantage&apos;s Villanueva de Gállego campus a &quot;strategic&quot; investment. Published in BOA #190 on 1 October 2025.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.boa.aragon.es/cgi-bin/EBOA/BRSCGI?CMD=VEROBJ&MLKOB=1413456710909" target="_blank">Source (BOA)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-permit">
<div class="tl-date">12 November 2025</div>
<div class="tl-title">Forestalia &quot;Proyecto Búfalo&quot; (3 sites, €12,048 M) approved</div>
<div class="tl-body">Aragón government approves Búfalo, three co-located datacenter + renewable-generation campuses in Magallón, Botorrita and Alfamén promoted by Aragonese renewable developer Forestalia. 601 MW total, no publicly disclosed anchor tenant.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.hoyaragon.es/articulo/noticias-aragon/luz-verde-boa-proyecto-bufalo-forestalia-centros-datos/20251202121828117704.html" target="_blank">Source (Hoy Aragón)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-permit">
<div class="tl-date">14 November 2025</div>
<div class="tl-title">Microsoft PIGA initial approval (ORDEN FOM/1520/2025)</div>
<div class="tl-body">The Aragón government initially approves the &quot;Región MSFT&quot; PIGA covering three campuses (La Muela, Villamayor de Gállego, Zaragoza), €5,356 M, 669 MW. 30 business days of public consultation begin.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.aragonhoy.es/fomento-vivienda-logistica-cohesion-territorial/aprobado-inicialmente-piga-microsoft-instalacion-tres-centros-datos-aragon-102741" target="_blank">Source (Aragón Hoy)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-permit">
<div class="tl-date">25 November 2025</div>
<div class="tl-title">BOA ORDEN PEJ/1656/2025 - Forestalia Búfalo formalised</div>
<div class="tl-body">Formal BOA order for the Búfalo declaration. Published 2 December 2025 as BOA20251202020. Includes the priority-project status and self-consumption energy allocation.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.boa.aragon.es/cgi-bin/EBOA/BRSCGI?CMD=VEROBJ&MLKOB=1424067523636" target="_blank">Source (BOA)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-expansion">
<div class="tl-date">December 2025</div>
<div class="tl-title">First Microsoft–Iberdrola PPA in Europe (150 MW)</div>
<div class="tl-body">Two long-term PPAs (150 MW total) tied to wind farms in Burgos and Cantabria. Microsoft&apos;s first PPA with Iberdrola in Europe, positioned as covering the Aragón datacenter build-out.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.iberdrola.com/sala-comunicacion/noticias/detalle/iberdrola-microsoft-refuerzan-alianza-ppa-150-mw-nuevas-soluciones-globales-ia" target="_blank">Source (Iberdrola)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-litigation">
<div class="tl-date">January 2026</div>
<div class="tl-title">9 groups file alegaciones against Microsoft PIGA</div>
<div class="tl-body">Ecologistas en Acción, ANSAR, No es sequía es saqueo, APUDEPA, and 5 neighbourhood/heritage associations file objections to the Microsoft PIGA during the public information period. Key grounds: energy demand exceeding total 2024 Aragón consumption; €140 M+ tax exemptions; felling of ~1,000 pines at Pinares de Venecia.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.ecologistasenaccion.org/355073/asociaciones-ecologistas-y-vecinales-presentan-alegaciones-contra-el-piga-de-microsoft-en-aragon/" target="_blank">Source (Ecologistas en Acción)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-scandal">
<div class="tl-date">28 January 2026</div>
<div class="tl-title">Box2Bit abandons Cariñena, moves to Épila (€3.9B)</div>
<div class="tl-body">Red Eléctrica excludes the Cariñena site from its 2025–2030 grid plan; Box2Bit relocates to Épila (project &quot;Epilon&quot;), €3,900 M, 150 → 520 MW. Yudigar takes over the freed Cariñena parcels. A textbook validation of the speculative-build risk thesis: a multi-billion-euro project stranded before construction.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.eldiario.es/aragon/economia/empresa-box2bit-invertira-3-900-millones-euros-centro-datos-epila-renunciar-carinena_1_12942482.html" target="_blank">Source (elDiario.es)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-scandal">
<div class="tl-date">3 March 2026</div>
<div class="tl-title">Operación Perserte, Guardia Civil detains Forestalia owner</div>
<div class="tl-body">The UCOMA (UCO&apos;s environmental unit) arrests Fernando Samper Rivas, owner of Forestalia (promoter of the €12B Búfalo datacenter project), as the alleged &quot;central hub&quot; from the private sector, plus former MITECO senior official Eugenio Domínguez. 12 simultaneous searches, 6 detentions. Charges: environmental prevarication, bribery, money laundering, criminal organisation. Investigators allege manipulated environmental permits.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.eldiario.es/aragon/politica/dueno-forestalia-exalto-cargo-transicion-ecologica-detenidos-presunta-corrupcion-renovables_1_13035201.html" target="_blank">Source (elDiario.es)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-permit">
<div class="tl-date">May 2026</div>
<div class="tl-title">PIGA Expansión AWS, third partial final approval</div>
<div class="tl-body">Aragón government approves the third partial phase of the AWS expansion PIGA. Villanueva de Gállego expansion (13 + 68 ha) and Walqa/Huesca (69 ha) plots cleared for construction.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://www.aragonhoy.es/fomento-vivienda-logistica-cohesion-territorial/aprobada-definitivamente-parte-piga-aws-iniciar-edificacion-nuevos-centros-datos-huesca-villanueva-gallego-104904" target="_blank">Source (Aragón Hoy)</a>
</div>

<div class="tl-event tag-litigation">
<div class="tl-date">2026</div>
<div class="tl-title">First datacenter lawsuit in Spain filed at TSJA</div>
<div class="tl-body">Ecologistas en Acción, represented by Fons de Defensa Ambiental, files an administrative-litigation appeal at the Tribunal Superior de Justicia de Aragón (TSJA) against the AWS PIGA. Co-plaintiffs: ANSAR, Amigos de la Tierra, Tunubesecamirio, Ingenieros Sin Fronteras, Plataforma en Defensa de los Paisajes de Teruel, RAPA, SEO/BirdLife.</div>
<a class="tl-src" href="https://climatica.coop/aragon-primer-litigio-centros-de-datos-espana-amazon/" target="_blank">Source (Climática)</a>
</div>
