Aragón Datacenter Map

Operators, investors, clients, energy/water, government permits — every datapoint cited.

How to read this map
  • Each dot is a datacenter project. Colour = operator type (see Legend).
  • Dot size = power capacity (MW) — bigger dot, more power.
  • Red ring = speculative (no confirmed tenant). Dashed red = litigation or objections.
  • Map layers add context: a 500 m heat plume, the equivalent solar-farm footprint, and NASA soil moisture.
  • Overlapping sites group into a numbered blue circle — click it to fan them out.
  • Click any dot for the full, cited record and its sources.
  • Watch the badges: PIGA fast-track, ICIO tax dispute, and litigation.

Illustrative, not the site’s actual supply: the land a solar farm alone would need to match this site’s electricity, drawn to scale. Most projects actually use a mix of solar and wind PPAs — wind needs far more spacing but far less built-up land. Assumes load factor 0.90, Aragón solar capacity factor ~0.20, ~1.5 ha/MWp (NREL); figures scale ~2× with these.

Live NASA SMAP soil-moisture (how much water is in the ground, ~9 km, updated every few days). Regional context, not site-scale — see the map legend for the colour scale. Click a site for the live reading at its location.
