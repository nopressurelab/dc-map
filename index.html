Each amber square is the solar farm needed to supply that site’s electricity, drawn to scale on the map. Assumes datacentre load factor 0.90, Aragón solar capacity factor ~0.20, ~1.5 ha/MWp (NREL). Figures scale ~2× with these assumptions.

Live Earth-observation layer (~5 km, updated every 10 days). Regional context, not site-scale. Areas with no colour aren't currently classified — expect the Combined Drought Indicator to be blank when there's no active drought. Click a site for the live reading at its location.
